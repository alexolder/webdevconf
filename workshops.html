Workshops

On Thursday afternoon between 12:00 and 17:00 we will be running a small number of workshops at the Watershed.

They are a great chance to learn something new as well as meeting new people who may also been at the conference on Friday.

Design Sytems Rock!

Hosted by Stu Robson

How we are creating websites and applications has moved on from building pages towards building a design system. Using style guides, pattern libraries and front-end frameworks to help create a product with consistency, efficiency, beauty and clarity.

Buy your ticket through Get Invited

In this workshop, you will learn and practice:

  • looking at the people that are potentially involved in using and maintaining a design system.
  • work out the various patterns, UI elements and layouts from a design.
  • working out and deciding the requirements for a design system
  • looking at various pattern libraries available and their pros and cons for your project
  • start using build tools to create some tasks that will help
  • learn how to build maintainable, reusable, modular code for your project(s)

This workshop is perfect for front-end developers and designers that are looking to add a better understanding and implementation of the designs and code that's used on their proejcts.

To be able to make the most from this workshop, you will need to bring a laptop with your preferred text editor, an email with more 'setup details' will be sent out nearer the time.

Product Design in 5 Days

Hosted by
Luke Murphy-Wearmouth

Whether you've built websites for companies, e-commerce platforms, apps or actual real-life products, you've had your hands in product design at some point. Product design isn't just about UX or UI, but about taking an idea, business goals and constraints and turning them into a functional product. Design Sprints are a great technique for validating product ideas and concepts with real user feedback in the matter of 5 days.

Buy your ticket through Get Invited

In this practical workshop you will learn:

  • The steps of a 5 day design sprint
  • Establishing how to solve the right problems
  • How to establish a productive and creative space
  • Group ideation without wasting time
  • How to plan user testing and recruit testees on the cheap
  • Techniques for high fidelity rapid prototyping

This workshop is perfect for product designers and front-end designers who are looking to add a layer of process to their work that actually works.

To be able to make the most of this workshop you will need to bring a laptop with your choice of design tools installed. An email will be sent out closer to the date with anything else that needs to be installed.
