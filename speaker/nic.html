Nicolas Alpi

Technical Director at CookiesHQ

Nicolas is the Director of CookiesHQ, a Bristol-based web app development company that frequently works with start-ups and entrepreneurs. Along with his wife, Nathalie, they have created a small, but perfectly formed multi-disciplinary team that specialises in taking on clients’ technical problems and offering a digital solution. Having enjoyed many years of back-end and front-end development, he now pretends to be the boss and finds himself interested in team processes and workflow improvements.

Talk: You're the boss now

From freelance to running an agency, dealing with growth, success, failure and mild burnout!

Have you ever wanted to quit your job and work on your own terms?

Maybe you're already a freelancer, but are looking to expand into something a bit more formal - you know, start a company with employees and everything?

If you’re at that point, then we’ve got some things to discuss. Let's talk about cash flow, building teams, gaining clients, hiring and firing, burning out, recovering, marketing, delegation, competition...and everything else that you need to consider when starting out on your own.
